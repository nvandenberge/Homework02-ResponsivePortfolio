About Me

My name is Nick and I am a Software Engineer currently living in Baltimore, MD. I have a Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Systems from Stevenson University. While working as a Software Support Specialist between 2015 - 2019 I found myself growing interest in how the software was developed. I began self-studying web development in my spare time and decided to officially commit to it in 2018 when I took an 8-week coding bootcamp in Baltimore. In 2019 I started developing part-time for the same organization I was working for by working on small bugs and features while maintaining my position as a Software Support Specialist. In 2020 I was promoted to full-time Software Engineer. 

After working as a Software Engineer for 6 months, I was provided me the opportunity to learn more about full-stack development by taking a 24-week coding bootcamp at Johns Hopkins University to learn more about back-end technologies interact with front-end. In my free time, I like to study full-stack development, exercise, and play video games with friends. I am very passionate about Web Development, and strive to better myself as a developer.
